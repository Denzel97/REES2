About Us

We Help Our Members To Grow Their Business

Rachuonyo East Entrepreneurs Savings and credit Cooperatibe Society Ltd (REES SACCO) was Registered in September 2018. This organization was established by entrepreneurs of Rachuonyo East sub county with the main objective of organizing and promoting the welfare and economic interest of its members.

To promote a saving culture, provide diverse, competitive and affordable financial services to socially and economically empower our members.

To be a leading SACCO in Kenya.
